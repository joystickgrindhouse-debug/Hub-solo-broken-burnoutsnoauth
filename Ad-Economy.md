# Ad-Economy System for Free Users

## Goal
The goal of this system is to create a sustainable revenue stream from free users without compromising user experience or retention.

## Free User Ad Rules
| Goal | Ads Required |
| :--- | :--- |
| Unlock raffle ticket earning for 1 day | 5 ads |
| Get 10 raffle tickets instantly | 10 ads |
| Double earned tickets for the next 24h | 15 ads |
| Boost/Bonus ticket wheel | 5 ads per spin |
| Skip ad cooldown for 1 hour | 10 ads |

## Economy Breakdown
- **Target Monthly Ads**: ~50–60 ads per active free user.
- **Estimated Ad Revenue**: ~$6.00 per month per user (based on $0.10 eCPM).

## Premium Value Proposition
- **Price**: $14.99/mo ($9.99 Founding Price).
- **Net Revenue**: ~$10+ per user after platform fees.
- **Core Benefits**:
    - No ads.
    - Unlimited raffle ticket earning.
    - Exclusive raffle access.
    - Bonus ticket multipliers.
    - Priority raffle entries.

## Upgrade Messaging
*"Watch ads to earn tickets, or unlock unlimited ticket earning and raffles for $9.99/month — less ads, more rewards, and exclusive raffle access!"*
